About the Challenge

Hi, I'm Harrison, and I'm 8 years old.

I've set myself an exciting challenge for 2026: to run a parkrun in Wiltshire every single month of the year. That's 12 parkruns in total!

I'm doing this to raise money for Wiltshire and Bath Air Ambulance, a charity that provides vital critical medical care by land and air across our communities. They're here when people need them most, and they rely entirely on donations to keep their lifesaving service flying.

Every donation, no matter how small, helps them continue their incredible work saving lives across Wiltshire and Bath.

Why Your Support Matters

Lifesaving Service

Wiltshire and Bath Air Ambulance provides critical medical care by land and air across Wiltshire and Bath. They respond to emergencies when every second counts.

Community Funded

As a registered charity, they don't receive government funding. They rely entirely on voluntary donations and support from our local communities.

Your Impact

Every donation helps keep their helicopters and critical care teams ready to respond. Your support truly saves lives.
